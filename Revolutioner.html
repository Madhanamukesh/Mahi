Mahendra singh Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni (MS Dhoni) is a legendary Indian cricketer known as "Captain Cool" for his calm leadership, explosive batting, and exceptional wicket-keeping, leading India to major ICC trophies (2007 T20 WC, 2011 ODI WC, 2013 Champions Trophy) and the Chennai Super Kings (CSK) to multiple IPL titles. From humble beginnings in Ranchi, he rose to become one of cricket's most successful captains, a prolific finisher, and a beloved figure known for his humility and unique style, playing for India and CSK in the IPL
